Synapse / 14 July 2020

Synapse provides an abstraction layer that enforces developers to follow SOLID principles and avoid common mistakes during the development process. It is based on keeping things SIMPLE and clear. The framework strongly encourages convention over configuration, while highlighting the criticality of structure.
